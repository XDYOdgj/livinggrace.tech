Hibbent Self-Cleaning Shower Head Combo

Step into a world of luxury and convenience with the Hibbent Self-Cleaning Shower Head Combo. This innovative set is crafted to transform your everyday shower into a spa-like retreat, combining powerful performance with effortless maintenance. The 10-inch rainfall shower head delivers a full-body, high-pressure rain spray, while the handheld model offers the flexibility to handle all your personal needs, whether it's shaving, brushing your teeth, or washing your hair. The 12-inch adjustable curved extension arm keeps the shower head in a perfect horizontal position, avoiding any contact with the unit and ensuring a steady, uninterrupted flow of water.

Elevate Your Bathroom with Hibbent's Shower System

Designed for both comfort and efficiency, the Hibbent combo features self-cleaning nozzles that automatically reset after each use, eliminating the hassle of clogs and ensuring crystal-clear water flow every time. The 3-way diverter allows you to control the water flow between the rainfall and handheld shower heads, giving you the freedom to customize your shower experience. All components are built with high-quality, corrosion-resistant materials, including a 71-inch stainless steel hose, ensuring long-lasting durability and a sleek, seamless look in your bathroom. With its matte black finish and easy installation guide, this combo is perfect for both modern homes and those looking to upgrade their shower system effortlessly.
