RexSoul Handheld Shower Head

Crafted for a smarter, healthier shower experience

Introducing the RexSoul handheld shower head, designed to enhance your daily shower with pure, healthy water. Our advanced 15-stage filter system removes chlorine, heavy metals, and other impurities, ensuring your skin and hair stay protected and nourished with every drop. This innovative filtration process also helps preserve the brightness of your hair and the softness of your skin, making it a favorite among those who value natural beauty and wellness.

With 10 customizable spray modes, including a soothing massage and a refreshing rain spray, the RexSoul offers versatile options to suit your mood and needs. Whether you're relaxing after a long day or preparing for a spa-like ritual, you'll find the perfect setting. The built-in pause switch allows you to save water effortlessly without disrupting the water temperature, delivering convenience and sustainability in one sleek design. Plus, the self-clean nozzles maintain consistent water pressure and prevent clogging, ensuring smooth and efficient performance over time.

Enjoy a more comfortable and relaxing shower with the RexSoul. Its powerful yet gentle 1.8 GPM flow rate ensures a satisfying experience without wasting water. For families and individuals who prioritize health, ease, and sustainable living, the RexSoul is the perfect upgrade to your bathroom routine.
