Elevate Your Bathroom with Hibbent All Metal Shower Combo

Transform your daily shower into a spa-like retreat with Hibbent's premium all-metal shower system. Designed for both comfort and longevity, this combo is built to last with high-quality SUS 304 stainless steel and solid brass components, offering rust-free durability that stands the test of time.

Featuring a 12x10'' rainfall shower head with air-in technology and 154 splash jets, this system delivers a luxurious rain spray experience that envelops you in a soothing, refreshing mist. The handheld shower wand adds flexibility, allowing you to adjust your shower angle and reach exactly where you need it, making every moment more personalized and enjoyable.

With a 16'' adjustable arc-shaped extension arm, you can customize the height and angle of your rainfall head, ensuring it stays in a horizontal position and prevents water from sloping over. The 3-way diverter valve makes it easy to switch between rainfall and hand-held modes without requiring additional tools or professional assistance.

This all-inclusive shower combo comes with everything you need for a seamless installation: 2 showerhead holders, adhesive brackets, washers, Teflon tape, and an easy-to-follow installation manual. Whether you're a homeowner seeking a luxury upgrade or a professional in the plumbing industry, Hibbent offers a reliable, stylish, and high-performing solution for your bathroom.
